<h1 className="subheading">Storybook</h1>
<p>
    Design System was created to help us build products for our customers more efficiently.
    Established to be the single source of truth for product teams working on ORIGIN products.
    Storybook Storybook Design System contains everything from Getting Started guides through to Core Components intended for adoption within each product.
    It enables product teams to design and develop with speed, consistency, and quality.
</p>
<h2>Objectives & Benefits</h2>
<h3>Consistency</h3>
<p>Create a common set of principles, resources, and components to improve consistency and end user experience across product.</p>
<p><b>Benefits</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>Creates a single source of truth for product teams</li>
  <li>Improves quality as a result of documented and applied standards</li>
  <li>Improves user experience as a result of consistent components, design interactions, and patterns</li>
</ul>
<h3>Efficiency</h3>
<p>Develop a unified system, with the appropriate governance applied, to increase the speed at which product teams produce new or improved components, and reduce duplication.</p>
<p><b>Benefits</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>Improves velocity for designers and developers working across projects</li>
  <li>Harnesses the collective power of multiple product teams to deliver more effectively</li>
  <li>Reduces duplication of work across product teams</li>
  <li>Frees up time to focus on innovation through new technology and frameworks</li>
</ul>
<h3>Collaboration</h3>
<p>Create an open, extensive, and evolving platform accessible for all stakeholders to support collaboration between disciplines, teams, and markets.</p>
<p><b>Benefits</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>Improves collaboration between disciplines through common tools and language</li>
  <li>Creates the foundation for scale, with other product teams</li>
  <li>Improves the onboarding experience for product team members</li>
</ul>
